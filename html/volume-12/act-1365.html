THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS AERIAL ROPEWAYS REGULATION, 1960

THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS AERIAL ROPEWAYS REGULATION, 1960

(BENGAL REGULATION II OF 1959)

[ 23rd December, 1960 ]

A

REGULATION

to apply the Bengal Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923, to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the Bengal Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923, to the Chittagong Hill-tracts;

NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of the Presidential Procalmation of the seventh day of October, 1958, and in exercise of all powers enabling him in that behalf, the 1[Government] is pleased to make the following Regulation,

namely:-

Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Regulation may be called the Chittagong Hill- tracts Aerial Ropeways Regulation, 1960.

    (2) It shall come into force at once

Application of Ben. Act VII of 1923 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The Bengal Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923 (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), and the rules made thereunder shall, subject to the following amendments, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts, namely:

Amendments

Amendment of the long title and the preamble

 

1. In the long title and the preamble of the said Act, for the word "Bengal" occurring twice, the words 1[Bangladesh] shall be subtituted.

Amendment of section 1 of the

Ben. Act VII of 1923

 

2. Sub-sections (2) and (3) of section 1 of the said Act shall be omitted.
Amendment of section 2 of the Ben. Act VII of 1923

3. In section 2 of the said Act,-

(a) in clause (1) for the words "an aerial ropeway", the words "an aerial ropeway or overhead monorail crane" shall be substituted;

(b) for clause (3), the following shall be substituted, namely:-

"(3) “Deputy Commissioner” means the Deputy Commissioner of the Chittagong Hill-tracts and includes any officer specially appointed by the 2[Government] to discharge the functions of a Deputy Commissioner under this Regulation;";

(c) for sub-clause (iv) of clause (9), the following shall be substituted, namely:-

"(iv) any company incorporated under the Companies Act, 1913."; and 

(d) sub-clause (v) of clause (9) shall be omitted.
