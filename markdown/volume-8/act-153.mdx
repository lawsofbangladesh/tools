**Date of Publication:** [ 25th July, 1930 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS the [Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1913](/act-101.html "Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1913"), does not apply to wakfs created before its enactment;AND WHEREAS it is expedient to validate such wakfs without infringing any rights contrary thereto which may have already accrued or been acquired; It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

1. This Act may be called the [Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1930](/act-153.html "Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1930").

### Act VI of 1913 to apply retrospectively

2. The [Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1913](/act-101.html "Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1913"), shall be deemed to apply to wakfs created before its commencement:Provided that nothing herein contained shall be deemed in any way to affect any right, title, obligation or liability already acquired, accrued or incurred before the commencement of this Act.
