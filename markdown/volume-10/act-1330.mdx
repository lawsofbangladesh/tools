**Date of Publication:** [ 4th June, 1942 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the [Merchandise Marks Act, 1889](/act-62.html "Merchandise Marks Act, 1889"), to the Chittagong Hill-Tracts;

It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

1. This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. V) REGULATION, 1942](/act-1330.html "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. V) REGULATION, 1942").

### Application of Act IV of 1889 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The [Merchandise Marks Act, 1889](/act-62.html "Merchandise Marks Act, 1889"), in so far as it is not inconsistent with the [Chittagong Hill-tracts Regulation, 1900](/act-1313.html "Chittagong Hill-tracts Regulation, 1900") or with any rules made thereunder for the time being in force, shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
