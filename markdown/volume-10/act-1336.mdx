## Sections/Articles

### Short title

1. This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. IV) REGULATION, 1943](/act-1336.html "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. IV) REGULATION, 1943").

### Application of Bengal Act I of 1885 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The [3](1)[\*\*\*] [Ferries Act, 1885](/act-54.html "Ferries Act, 1885"), with the exception of section 3 of the said Act, shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
