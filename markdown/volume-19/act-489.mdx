**Date of Publication:** [ 12th July, 1975 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to give the expiry of the Bangladesh (Restoration of Evacuee Property) Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 13 of 1972), the effect of repeal of an Act;

It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Act may be called the [Bangladesh (Restoration of Evacuee Property) (Effect of Expiry) Act, 1975](/act-489.html "Bangladesh (Restoration of Evacuee Property) (Effect of Expiry) Act, 1975").(2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 25th day of March, 1975.

### Effect of Expiry, P.O. No. 13 of 1972

2. It is hereby declared that the expiry of the Bangladesh (Restoration of Evacuee Property) Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 13 of 1972), on the 25th day of March, 1975 shall have the effect of repeal of an Act as provided in section 6 of the [General Clauses Act, 1897](/act-73.html "General Clauses Act, 1897") (X of 1897).

### Repeal

3. The Bangladesh (Restoration of Evacuee Property) (Effect of Expiry) Ordinance, 1975 (XVI of 1975) is hereby repealed.
