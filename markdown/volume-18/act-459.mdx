**Date of Publication:** [ 5th February, 1974 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) Ordinance, 1966 (E.P. Ord. IV of 1966);It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Act may be called the [Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) (Repeal) Act, 1974](/act-459.html "Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) (Repeal) Act, 1974").(2) It shall come into force at once.

### Repeal

2. The Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) Ordinance, 1966 (E.P. Ord. IV of 1966) is hereby repealed.
