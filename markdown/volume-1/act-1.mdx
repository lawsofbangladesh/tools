## Preamble

## Sections/Articles

### Power to create new Districts

It shall be lawful for the [2](1)[\*\*\*] Government, by notification in the official Gazette, to create new districts in any part of Bangladesh.[3](2)[Government may, from time to time, alter the limits of the existing districts in any part of Bangladesh].
