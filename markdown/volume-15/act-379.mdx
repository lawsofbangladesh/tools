  Clause (1) was substituted by section 2 of The Bangladesh Inland Water Transport Corporation (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (XXVII of 1976)

  The figure “4500,000” were substituted for the figure “500,000” by section 2 of The Bangladesh Inland Water Transport Corporation (Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 (VII of 1979)
