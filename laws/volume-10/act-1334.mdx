**Date of Publication:** [ 18th March, 1943 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the [Explosive Substances Act, 1908](/act-87.html "Explosive Substances Act, 1908"), to the Chittagong Hill-tracts;

It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

1. This Regulation may be called the Chittagong Hill-tracts Laws Regulation, 1943.

### Application of Act VI of 1908 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The [Explosive Substances Act, 1908](/act-87.html "Explosive Substances Act, 1908"), shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
