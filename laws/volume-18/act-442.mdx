**Date of Publication:** [ 6th January, 1973 ]

## Preamble

NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of paragraph 3 of the Fourth Schedule to the Constitution of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh and in exercise of all powers enabling him in that behalf, the President is pleased to make the following Order:-

## Sections/Articles

### 8A

[6](1)[8A. The Institute shall ensure that all members of the Institute shall comply with the Financial Reporting Standards and Auditing Standards issued by the Financial Reporting Council (FRC) pursuant to section 40 of the Financial Reporting Act, 2015.]
