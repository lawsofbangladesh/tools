**Date of Publication:** [ 23rd December, 1960 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the Bengal [Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923](/act-130.html "Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923"), to the Chittagong Hill-tracts;

NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of the Presidential Procalmation of the seventh day of October, 1958, and in exercise of all powers enabling him in that behalf, the 1[Government] is pleased to make the following Regulation,

namely:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Regulation may be called the Chittagong Hill- tracts Aerial Ropeways Regulation, 1960.

    (2) It shall come into force at once

### Application of Ben. Act VII of 1923 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The Bengal [Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923](/act-130.html "Aerial Ropeways Act, 1923") (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), and the rules made thereunder shall, subject to the following amendments, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts, namely:

_Amendments_

|                                                    |                                                                                                                                                      |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Amendment of the long title and the preamble       | **1.** In the long title and the preamble of the said Act, _for_ the word "Bengal" occurring twice, the words [1][Bangladesh] shall be _subtituted_. |
| Amendment of section 1 of the Ben. Act VII of 1923 | **2.** Sub-sections _(2)_ and _(3)_ of section 1 of the said Act shall be _omitted_.                                                                 |

### Amendment of section 2 of the Ben. Act VII of 1923

3. In section 2 of the said Act,-

(a) in clause (1) for the words "an aerial ropeway", the words "an aerial ropeway or overhead monorail crane" shall be substituted;

(b) for clause (3), the following shall be substituted, namely:-

"(3) “Deputy Commissioner” means the Deputy Commissioner of the Chittagong Hill-tracts and includes any officer specially appointed by the [2](1)[Government] to discharge the functions of a Deputy Commissioner under this Regulation;";

_(c) for_ sub-clause _(iv)_ of clause (9), the following shall be _substituted_, namely:-

"_(iv)_ any company incorporated under the Companies Act, 1913."; and

_(d)_ sub-clause _(v)_ of clause (9) shall be _omitted_.
