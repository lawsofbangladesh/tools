**Date of Publication:** [ 26th July, 1952 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

 1. This Regulation may be called the Chittagong Hill- tracts (Laws) Regulation, 1952. 

### Application of certain enactments to the Chittagong Hill tracts

 2. The enactment specified in the Schedule annexed hereto shall, subject to the amendments mentioned in the fourth column thereof, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts and shall be deemed to have applied to the said tracts with effect from the 21st June, 1949. 

### Repeal

 3. The Chittagong Hill-tracts  (Laws)  Regulation, 1949, is hereby repealed.
