**Date of Publication:** [ 22th January, 1951 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

  (1) This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS SARAIS REGULATION, 1951](/laws/act-1345 "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS SARAIS REGULATION, 1951") .       (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.    

### Application of Act XXII of 1867 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

    2.     The [Sarais Act, 1867](/laws/act-18 "Sarais Act, 1867") , shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
