**Date of Publication:** [ 2th February, 1955 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

 1. This Regulation may be called the Chittagong Hill- tracts Cinematograph Regulation, 1954. 

### Application of Act II of 1918 to the Chittagong Hill-tracts

 2. The [Cinematograph Act, 1918](/laws/act-108 "Cinematograph Act, 1918") , together with Rules made thereunder, shall, subject to the modification shown below, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts:-   Modification.   Sub-section (3) of section 1 shall be omitted.
