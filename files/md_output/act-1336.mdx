**Date of Publication:** [ 23th December, 1943 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the <sup>[[2]](#footnote-2)</sup> [***] [Ferries Act, 1885](/laws/act-54 "Ferries Act, 1885") , to the Chittagong Hill-tracts; It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

 1. This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. IV) REGULATION, 1943](/laws/act-1336 "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. IV) REGULATION, 1943") . 

### Application of Bengal Act I of 1885 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

 2. The <sup>[[3]](#footnote-3)</sup> [\* \* \*] [Ferries Act, 1885](/laws/act-54 "Ferries Act, 1885") , with the exception of section 3 of the said Act, shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
