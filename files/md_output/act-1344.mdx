**Date of Publication:** [ 12th September, 1943 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

 1. (1) This Regulation may be called [THE EXCLUDED AND PARTIALLY EXCLUDED AREAS LAWS REGULATION, 1950](/laws/act-1344 "THE EXCLUDED AND PARTIALLY EXCLUDED AREAS LAWS REGULATION, 1950") .      (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.    

### Application of Bengal Act III of 1931 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts and to the partially excluded areas of the Mymenshingh district

   2. The     <sup>[[4]](#footnote-4)</sup> [\* \* \*]     [State Aid to Industries Act, 1931](/laws/act-154 "State Aid to Industries Act, 1931") , shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts and to the partially excluded areas of the Mymensingh district.
