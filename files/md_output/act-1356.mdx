**Date of Publication:** [ 15th August, 1953 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

 1. (1) This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS (TARIFF AND CUSTOMS) REGULATION, 1952](/laws/act-1356 "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS (TARIFF AND CUSTOMS) REGULATION, 1952") .  (2) It shall come into force at once. 

### Application of certain Central Acts to the Chittagong Hill-tracts

 2. (1) The enactments specified in Schedule I annexed hereto shall, subject to the amendments mentioned in the fourth column thereof, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.  (2) The enactment specified in Schedule II annexed hereto shall, to the extent mentioned in the fourth column thereof, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
