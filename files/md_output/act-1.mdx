**Date of Publication:** [ 11th September, 1836 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Power to create new Districts

It shall be lawful for the <sup>[[2]](#footnote-2)</sup> [\* \* \*] Government, by notification in the official Gazette, to create new districts in any part of Bangladesh.   <sup>[[3]](#footnote-3)</sup> [Government may, from time to time, alter the limits of the existing districts in any part of Bangladesh].
