**Date of Publication:** [ 14th May, 1957 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

 1. (1) This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS MOTOR VEHICLES REGULATION, 1957](/laws/act-1361 "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS MOTOR VEHICLES REGULATION, 1957") .  (2) It shall come into force at once. 

### Application of certain Acts to the Chittagong Hill-tracts

 2. The enactments specified in the Schedule below together with all rules made thereunder, shall, subject to the amendments mentioned in the fourth column thereof, apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
