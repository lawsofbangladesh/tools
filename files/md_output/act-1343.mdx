**Date of Publication:** [ 27th March, 1950 ]

## Sections/Articles

### Short title and commencement

 1. (1) This Regulation may be called the <sup>[[2]](#footnote-2)</sup> [\* \* \*] Partially Excluded Areas Laws Regulation, 1950.    (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.  

### Application of Act II of 1947 to the partially excluded areas of the Mymensingh district

   2. The [Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947](/laws/act-217 "Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947") , shall apply to the partially excluded areas of the Mymensingh district.
