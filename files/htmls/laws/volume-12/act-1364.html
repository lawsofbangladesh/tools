THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS EMPLOYMENT (RECORD OF SERVICES) REGULATION, 1959

THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS EMPLOYMENT (RECORD OF SERVICES) REGULATION, 1959

(EAST PAKISTAN REGULATION NO. II OF 1959)

[ 28th December, 1959 ]

A

REGULATION

to apply the Employment (Record of Services) Act, 1951 to the Chittagong Hill Tracts.

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the Employment (Record of Services) Act, 1951 to the Chittagong Hill-tracts;

Now, therefore, in pursuance of the Presidential Proclamation of the seventh day of October, 1958, and in exercise of all powers enabling it in that behalf, the 1 [Government] is pleased to make the following Regulation, namely:-

Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Regulation may be called THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS EMPLOYMENT (RECORD OF SERVICES) REGULATION, 1959 .

(2) It shall come into force at once.

Application of Act XIX of 1952 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The Employment (Record of Services) Act, 1951 (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), and the rules made thereunder shall, subject to the following amendments apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts, namely:-

Amendments

Amendment of section 1 of Act No. XIX of 1952 Sub-section (2) and (4) of section 1 of the said Act shall be omitted.

  • 1
    The word "Government" was replaced, for the words "Governor of East Pakistan" by Article 9 of the Bangladesh (Adaptation of Existing Laws) Order, 1972 (President's Order No. 48 of 1972).
